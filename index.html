实验室简介

Biography

Zheng obtained his Ph.D. in computer science and technology from Tianjin University in 2009, and became a Lecturer in the College of Software, Tianjin University Software College afterwards. In 2013, he was promoted to Associate Professor. Now he is currently working as a associate professor in College of Intelligence and Computing of Tianjin University. His main research interests include image and video processing, saliency detection, hyperspectral imaging and machine learning based object detection and classification. He has published around 50 peer-reviewed papers in internationally renowned journals and conferences, including top ones such as IEEE Transaction on Cybernetics, ACM Multimedia, IJCAI and Neurocomputing.

王征，天津大学人工智能学院副教授，天津大学机器学习与数据挖掘团队（负责人胡清华）成员，硕导，曾获得北洋学者青年骨干教师、天津大学我心目中的好导师等荣誉称号。曾赴法国国家信息与自动化研究所（INRIA）访学一年（2007），赴美国南卡罗莱纳大学访学一年（2016）。研究方向为人工智能、计算机视觉、工业智能无损检测、电力系统优化软件和预测算法和计算机水墨画仿真。实验室成立的3年内，发表论文三十余篇，其中SCI检索论文二十多篇，包括IEEE Transaction on Cybernetics、ACM Multimedia、IJCAI等高级期刊和会议。
